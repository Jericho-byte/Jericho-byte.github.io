Screenprinting Project

During the middle of the school year we were assigned a prject where we had to make a design in order to combat some sort of problem. the problem could've been something serious like climate change or something as unserious as school lunch. My design was based off of a problem that had had a big affect in my life before.

First Draft Of Design

Can you guess what problem my design is supposed to represent juse based off of its first draft? Well the truth is i probably couldn't either if i were you. it just looks a little weird doesnt it? Well thats actually the whole point of the design although im sure you might have caught onto that by now. The problem I chose to represent was people all acting the same and nobody choosing to be true to themselves. Even if it requires you to be a little weird its still better to stay true to yourself. And the way I chose to represent that was me being a weirdo and sticking my tongue out.
