Screenprinting Project

During the middle of the school year we were assigned a prject where we had to make a design in order to combat some sort of problem. the problem could've been something serious like climate change or something as unserious as school lunch. My design was based off of a problem that had had a big affect in my life before.

First Draft Of Design

Can you guess what problem my design is supposed to represent just based off of its first draft? Well the truth is i probably couldn't either if i were you. it just looks a little weird doesnt it? The problem I chose to represent was people all acting the same and nobody choosing to be true to themselves. And the way I chose to represent that was me being a weirdo and sticking my tongue out.

Screenprinting Frame

After our class made all of our designs we had to make our frames to actually hold our art. my frame was actually small in comparison to other frames but it held my art just fine. Albeit a little crookedly.

Final Design Print

The art we held in our frames actually had little wholes cut in it so that paint could flow through onto things like paper, fabric and cardboard. so after creating the frame we all got our paint and created our final designs. Here is mine

21st Century Skill Reflection

In the New Haven school system there are skills that every student is required to grow proficient in. These skills are, Problem Solving and Critical Thinking, Accessing and Analyzing Information, Communication and Collaboration, Creativity and Innovation, Initiative, Self-Direction and Accountability, and Citizenship and Responsibility.I believe the place I've grown most in is creativity and innovation because in all of my designs and processes even for things not in this class I've learned to be completely unique. However I've also grown in areas like communicsastion and collaboration, and now not only can I communicate with my peers better I'm less scared to ask a teacher for help.
